## Rating Categories

### ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Excellent (5 stars)
- Smooth cross-chain transfers with no issues
- Fast transaction times
- Excellent user experience
- Great documentation and support

### ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Good (4 stars)  
- Successful transfers with minor hiccups
- Generally good experience
- Most features work as expected

### ⭐⭐⭐ Average (3 stars)
- Transfers work but with some friction
- Room for improvement in UX or speed
- Some features could be better

### ⭐⭐ Below Average (2 stars)
- Encountered significant issues
- Difficult to use or slow
- Need considerable improvements

### ⭐ Poor (1 star)
- Major problems or failures
- Very difficult to use
- Critical issues that need immediate attention

**Note**: If you rate us 2 stars or below, please contact support@wormhole.com so we can help resolve your issues immediately.
